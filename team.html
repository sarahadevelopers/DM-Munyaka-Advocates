Principal & Managing Partner

Advocate David Munyaka

LLB (Hons) Moi University | Postgraduate Diploma, Kenya School of Law (KSL) | Advocate, High Court of Kenya

As the founder and lead strategist of D.M. Munyaka & Company Advocates, Adv. Munyaka oversees all high-stakes litigation, complex conveyancing matters, and corporate advisory. With a foundation built on rigorous academic excellence from Moi University and the Kenya School of Law, he ensures that every legal brief produced by the firm meets the highest standards of integrity and precision.

Adv. Munyaka is widely recognized for his calm courtroom demeanour, strategic negotiation skills, and deep understanding of the Kenyan legal landscape. He is an active member of the Law Society of Kenya (LSK) and regularly appears before the High Court, Environment and Land Court, and Employment and Labour Relations Court.
