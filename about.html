The Principal

D.M. Munyaka

LLB (Hons) Moi University • Dip. Law (KSL) • Advocate of the High Court

Mr. Munyaka's journey in law began with a passion for justice and a deep respect for Kenya's legal framework. After earning his Bachelor of Laws with honours from Moi University, he proceeded to the Kenya School of Law where he obtained his Postgraduate Diploma, emerging as a sharp, ethically driven advocate.

He has since built a reputation for meticulous preparation in civil litigation, conveyancing, and commercial advisory. Known for his calm courtroom demeanour and strategic negotiation skills, Mr. Munyaka is regularly retained by corporate entities and high-net-worth individuals across Machakos, Kitui, and Nairobi.
