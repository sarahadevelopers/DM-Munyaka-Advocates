D.M. Munyaka | Managing Partner

Founded on the principles of academic rigor and local insight, D.M. Munyaka & Company Advocates provides a sophisticated legal bridge for clients in Machakos and across the nation.

Our Principal, Mr. Munyaka, brings a prestigious academic background, having earned his Bachelor of Laws (LLB Hons) from Moi University. This foundation was further refined at the Kenya School of Law (KSL), where he attained his Post-Graduate Diploma in Law, solidifying his expertise in the Kenyan legal landscape.
