Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common legal inquiries

How do I initiate a legal consultation?

Call +254 724 327060, email info@dmmunyakaadv.co.ke, or visit our offices at Anam Annex Building, Suite 1, opposite Machakos Stadium.

What are the costs for property conveyancing?

Legal fees follow the Advocates Remuneration Order. We provide a transparent breakdown during your first meeting — no hidden charges.

Can you handle cases outside Machakos?

Yes. We represent clients in courts across Kenya, including Nairobi, Kitui, Makueni, and Embu.

How long does succession (probate) take?

Duration depends on estate complexity. Our expertise streamlines the process efficiently.

Do you offer Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)?

Absolutely. We prioritize mediation and arbitration as cost-effective alternatives to litigation.

Is the firm compliant with LSK regulations?

Yes. We are active members of the Law Society of Kenya in good standing.
